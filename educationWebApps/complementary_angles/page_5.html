Complementary Angles Exploration

Follow the worksheet to explore the relationship between complementary angles and trigonometric functions

Good job! Take a minute to read and review what we learned by doing these activities. It may be a good idea to write these down in your notes.
  • Complementary angles are two angles that sum to 90°
  • If angle A + angle B = 90° and angle A + angle C = 90°, then B = C
  • sin(x)=cos(90-x) and cos(x)=sin(90-x)
